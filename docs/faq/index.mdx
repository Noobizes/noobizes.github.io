    <summary>Why use the blockchain ?</summary>
    <p>
      The blockchain{' '}
      <strong>ensure a decentralization and an immutability</strong> of the
      data, it also allows to obtain a <strong>traceability over time</strong>.
      With <strong>Noobizes</strong> we aim to{' '}
      <strong>bring automatisation to the esport field</strong>, but also a{' '}
      <strong>trustful and secure environment</strong> for gamers.
      <br />
      The <strong>storage in blockchain</strong> then allows to obtain a
      traceability <strong>of the results and the various tournaments</strong>,
      which then establishes a <strong>Track record</strong> allowing to certify
      your victories and defeats. <br />
      This <strong>Track record</strong> would be a sure way to <strong>certify your CV in esport through your entire life</strong>
    </p>

    <summary>What is the difference between $NBZ & $sNBZ ?</summary>
    <p>
      The stable part allows to :
      <ul>
        <li>Onboard gamers by ramp solution (fiat/stable)</li>
        <li>Play challenges against other players/teams</li>
        <li>Participate/create tournament</li>
        <li>Pool betting (futur feature)</li>
        <li>Withdraw to $NBZ or Stablecoin (fiat if possible)</li>
        <li>
          Generate fee to buyback $NBZ for stacking and increase our reserve for
          operations
        </li>
        <li>Avoid exposition to crypto market</li>
      </ul>
      The Utility part allows to :<ul>
        <li>Mint your Member Card (subscription)</li>
        <li>Buy bonus for Member Card</li>
        <li>Buy event ticket</li>
        <li>Secondary market for Trophy</li>
        <li>Participate in governance</li>
        <li>Stake your token for interest rate</li>
        <li>Cash price for custom tournament</li>
        <li>Token burning</li>
      </ul>
    </p>
